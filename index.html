About

Dr. Anthony Eversole is a voice teacher and acting coach based in Salt Lake City, specializing in adult and pediatric vocal pedagogy across classical, musical theatre, and commercial styles. He is a former university voice professor with faculty experience at Oregon State University and the Crane School of Music at SUNY Potsdam.

As a baritone, he has performed opera and musical theatre with companies across the country — including the Maryland Opera Studio, Ash Lawn Opera, Castleton Festival, and Utah Lyric Opera — and appears regularly as an oratorio soloist. He holds the Doctor of Musical Arts from the University of Maryland.
