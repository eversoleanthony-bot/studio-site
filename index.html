The Teacher

Dr. Anthony Eversole is a voice teacher, stage director, acting coach, and former university voice professor who has performed musical theatre and opera with companies across the country.

Doctor of Musical Arts, University of Maryland. Faculty experience at Oregon State University and the Crane School of Music. Baritone credits from Sweeney Todd to Don Giovanni.

What Students Say
