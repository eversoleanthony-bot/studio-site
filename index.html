Voice Studio

Individualized vocal training to stabilize technique, reduce physical tension, and build a voice you can rely on.

— Evidence-Based Mechanics Train with a motor learning approach grounded in voice science, so coordination becomes predictable and repeatable.
— Text-First Exploration Work from the text outward, shaping technique and character together so both serve the storytelling.
— Built to Disappear Establish coordination that's wired in, freeing your attention for character and artistry.

Role & Audition Prep

Focused prep for singers building a role or preparing for an audition.

— Clear Choices Break down the script and score line by line, so every moment has a reason behind it instead of a vague feeling.
— Beat-by-Beat Focus Trade fidgeting and nervous habits for eye focus and gesture shaped by what your character is thinking, beat by beat.
— Rooted, Not Rote Line and phrase choices built from your character's wants and obstacles, so they stay fresh even on repeat.

The Teacher

Dr. Anthony Eversole combines functional vocal mechanics with real-world stage experience.

He's taught independent studio students, K-12 music classes, and university voice, musical theatre, and opera programs, on top of an active performing career.

Doctor of Musical Arts, University of Maryland  •  Master of Music, University of Oklahoma  •  Bachelor of Music, Utah State University.
