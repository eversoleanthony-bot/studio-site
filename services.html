Services

Direction, Conducting
& Production

Professional engagement for productions, organizations, and creative projects. For private voice and acting instruction, see The Studio.

01

Stage Direction

Opera · Musical Theatre

Direction for opera and musical theatre productions at all levels — from academic workshops and youth conservatories to community and semi-professional companies. The work is rooted in a strong point of view, clear storytelling, and a deep respect for music and text.

As a performer and voice teacher, Eversole brings an unusual practical literacy to the rehearsal room — an understanding of how the voice works, what singers need, and how to build a production that serves the music without sacrificing the drama.

  • Opera and music theatre direction
  • Workshop and showcase productions
  • Movement and physical staging
  • Collaboration with music directors and choreographers
  • Youth and academic productions
Inquire
02

Music Direction

Musical Theatre · Opera

Music direction for musical theatre and opera productions at all levels. Strong keyboard skills, fluency across a wide repertoire, and a collaborative approach to working with directors, choreographers, and production teams.

Experienced working with singers from early beginners through professional casts — building ensembles, shaping scores, and creating the musical conditions in which a production can breathe.

  • Music direction for musical theatre and opera
  • Pit conducting and keyboard direction
  • Cast music preparation and rehearsal
  • Score study and adaptation
  • Youth and community productions welcome
Inquire
03

Choral Conducting

Ensemble · Choral

Conducting for choral ensembles in academic, community, and professional contexts. Work spans sacred and secular repertoire, from Renaissance polyphony through contemporary choral music, oratorio, and concert programming.

Particular interest in building ensemble sound, refining intonation and blend, and developing singers' individual technique within the choral context.

  • Community and academic choral directing
  • Concert programming and repertoire selection
  • Sectional coaching and vocal development
  • Oratorio and large ensemble preparation
Inquire
04

Music Production

Recording · Production

Music production services for singers, ensembles, and performing arts projects. From demo recordings and audition materials to fully produced recordings for release or archival purposes.

A deep understanding of the voice and performance practice informs every project — the goal is always a recording that captures what the performer actually does, rather than a technical artifact.

  • Vocal recording and production
  • Audition and demo recording
  • Ensemble and small ensemble recording
  • Arrangement and orchestration for recording
