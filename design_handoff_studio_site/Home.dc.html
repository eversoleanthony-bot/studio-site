The Teacher
Dr. Anthony Eversole is a voice teacher, acting coach, and director, a former university voice professor who has performed musical theatre and opera with companies across the country.
Doctor of Musical Arts, University of Maryland. Faculty experience at Oregon State University and the Crane School of Music. Baritone credits from Sweeney Todd to Don Giovanni.
