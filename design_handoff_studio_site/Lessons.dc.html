Common Questions
Do I need any experience to start?
No. Roughly half the studio's students are complete beginners; the other half are working toward a specific role or audition. Lessons are built around where you are.
What ages do you teach?
Ages 12 and up. A parent or guardian attends the consultation for students under 18.
Online or in person?
Both. Lessons run over Zoom for students anywhere, or in-studio in Salt Lake City if you're local.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule?
Reschedule with at least 24 hours' notice at no charge. See full policies on the Terms page.
