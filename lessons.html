The Studio

Lessons & Coaching

Private voice instruction, acting coaching, and audition preparation — in person in Salt Lake City and online worldwide. All ages, all levels, all three major traditions.

01
Voice & Performance Technique

One-on-one instruction built on evidence-based vocal technique: breath management, registration, resonance, and stylistic fluency across musical theatre, CCM, and classical repertoire. Acting coaching and singing-actor integration included as the work demands.

Musical Theatre CCM Classical Acting & Text
  • •Weekly 60- or 45-minute lessons, online or in person
  • •Repertoire planning aligned to your goals and timeline
  • •Practice recordings and written lesson notes after every session

02
Preparation & Goal Work

Targeted preparation for what is on your calendar. Bring the role, the audition, or the deadline. Work covers material selection, cuts, mock auditions, character research, and the specific demands of college programs, community productions, or professional houses.

Audition Prep Role Preparation College Programs
  • •Repertoire selection and cutting for the specific room
  • •Mock auditions with structured, honest feedback
  • •Character and score analysis for role and scene preparation
