The Studio

Teaching & Coaching

Evidence-based voice instruction, acting coaching, and audition preparation for students of all ages — online via Zoom and in person in Salt Lake City.

01

Private Voice Lessons

Online · In Person

One-on-one voice instruction for singers of all ages and experience levels, from early beginners through pre-professional students. Work is grounded in evidence-based vocal technique — combining anatomical understanding of the voice with sensory feedback to help students develop lasting self-diagnostic skills.

Repertoire spans classical, musical theatre, and commercial styles. Lessons are tailored to each student's voice, goals, and performance context — whether that's a recital, an audition, a production, or the ongoing development of a healthy, durable technique.

  • Technique: breath, registration, resonance, range
  • Repertoire selection and coaching
  • Diction in English, Italian, German, French
  • Vocal health and longevity
  • Performance preparation and stage delivery

Currently accepting new students online via Zoom. In-person availability in Salt Lake City by arrangement.

02

Acting Coaching

Online · In Person

Private coaching for actors and singing actors at any stage of training or career. The work integrates vocal technique with character connection, emotional specificity, and genuine ownership of the material — developing performers who can tell a story, not just execute it.

Sessions can focus on a specific role, a recurring challenge in performance, or ongoing scene study. Equally applicable to spoken drama and musical theatre.

  • Scene study and text analysis
  • Character development and backstory
  • Objective, obstacle, and action work
  • Physical and vocal expressiveness
  • Production coaching and role preparation

03

Audition Prep

Online · In Person

Focused preparation for specific auditions — college programs, conservatories, summer intensives, community and professional productions. The goal is not a polished performance of the work, but a confident, specific, and repeatable audition.

Sessions cover material selection, repertoire coaching, mock auditions, and the practical skills of walking into a room and making an impression quickly.

  • Repertoire selection strategy
  • 16- and 32-bar cut preparation
  • College and conservatory portfolio coaching
  • Mock auditions with feedback
  • Accompanist preparation and cut marking

04

Workshops & Group Clinics

In Person · By Arrangement

Voice and acting clinics for schools, studios, youth programs, and performing arts organizations. Sessions are designed to be immediately practical — students leave with specific tools they can apply in rehearsal and performance.

Available as single-session workshops or multi-week residencies. Topics are customized to the group and context.

  • Vocal health and technique fundamentals
  • Audition technique and repertoire
  • Musical theatre performance practice
  • Stage presence and physical expressiveness
  • Scene study and text work

05

Stage Direction

Opera · Musical Theatre

Direction for opera and musical theatre productions at all levels — from youth programs and community companies to semi-professional and professional productions. The work is rooted in a strong point of view, clear storytelling, and a deep respect for the music and text.

Past productions span a wide repertoire, from verismo opera and Brecht to golden-age and contemporary musical theatre.

  • Opera: Leoncavallo, Menotti, Donizetti, and others
  • Musical Theatre: Sondheim, Strouse, and others
  • Stage direction for workshop and showcase productions
  • Movement and physical staging
  • Collaboration with music directors and choreographers
