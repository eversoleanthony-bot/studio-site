Navigation Notes

  • Desktop nav (4 items): Lessons · About · Journal · Get Started (sage button). Tight, focused, no decision fatigue.
  • Mobile nav (6 items): Adds On Stage and For Organizations below the core three. Get Started as a button at the bottom.
  • Footer (6 links): Lessons · About · On Stage · Journal · For Organizations · Get Started.
  • On Stage is not in the desktop nav — it's a trust-builder, not a funnel page. Accessed from the About page, the homepage, and the footer.
  • For Organizations is off the main nav entirely. It serves a different audience (companies, schools, programs) and has a different tone. Linked from About and footer.
  • Journal posts are children of the Journal page. Category filtering happens on the Journal index; individual posts have prev/next navigation and a back link.
  • Every page ends with a CTA leading to Get Started. The student path is: Home → Lessons → Get Started. Secondary pages always offer a way back into the funnel.
