Privacy Policy

Anthony Eversole ("we," "us") respects your privacy. This page explains, in plain terms, what information we collect when you visit this site or book a lesson, and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you book a lesson or send an inquiry, we collect your name, email, and any details you share about your goals or production. Payment is processed by our booking provider (currently My Music Staff); we do not store your card details ourselves.

How We Use It

  • To schedule and run your lessons or sessions
  • To send confirmations, reminders, and invoices
  • To reply to inquiries about direction, workshops, or clinics
  • To send occasional studio-journal emails, only if you subscribe

What We Don't Do

We don't sell your information, and we don't share it beyond the tools that run the studio (booking and payment processing, email).

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out through the inquiry form or at the email address on your booking confirmation.
