Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Anthony Eversole ("we," "us") respects your privacy. This page explains, in plain terms, what information we collect when you visit this site or book a session, and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you book a session or send an inquiry, we collect your name, email, and any details you share about your goals or production. Individual session payments are processed by Cal.com. Bundle payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store your card details.

How We Use It

  • To schedule and run your sessions
  • To process payments and send confirmations
  • To send reminders and follow-ups related to your booking
  • To reply to inquiries about direction, workshops, or clinics
  • To send occasional studio updates, only if you subscribe

What We Don't Do

We don't sell your information, and we don't share it beyond the tools that run the studio: Cal.com (scheduling), Stripe (payments), and email.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out through the inquiry form or at the email address on your booking confirmation.
